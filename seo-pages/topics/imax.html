Should You See Disclosure Day in IMAX?

IMAX

Confirmed for Premium Large Format Release

IMAX Confirmation

Universal Pictures has confirmed that Disclosure Day will have an IMAX release alongside standard theatrical showings. This suggests significant sequences were either filmed with IMAX cameras or specifically formatted for the expanded aspect ratio.

Standard vs. IMAX

Standard Theater

  • 2.39:1 aspect ratio
  • Standard sound mix
  • Regular screen size
  • Lower ticket price

IMAX Experience

  • 1.90:1 expanded ratio (26% more image)
  • IMAX 12-channel sound
  • 70+ foot screens
  • Premium pricing (~$20-25)

Why IMAX for Disclosure Day?

Based on the subject matter and Spielberg's history, IMAX makes sense for:

  • Scale of revelation — The moment of disclosure deserves massive presentation
  • Intimate moments too — IMAX captures facial detail for Emily Blunt's performance
  • Janusz Kaminski's cinematography — His visual style benefits from larger format
  • John Williams' score — IMAX sound systems do justice to orchestral scores

Our Verdict

Yes, see it in IMAX — For a Spielberg event film with John Williams' potentially final score, the IMAX premium is worth it for the full experience.

Other Premium Formats

Beyond IMAX, Disclosure Day will likely be available in:

  • Dolby Cinema (Dolby Vision + Dolby Atmos)
  • 4DX (motion seats, effects)
  • ScreenX (270-degree screens)
  • Standard digital projection
