Disclosure Day Official Trailer

Trailer Breakdown

The teaser trailer for Disclosure Day dropped December 16, 2025, attached to screenings of Avatar: Fire and Ash. Here's what we see:

Key Moments

  • Emily Blunt as The Meteorologist — delivering a weather report when something takes over
  • The Possession — Blunt's character speaks in alien clicks, unable to control herself
  • Josh O'Connor as The Whistleblower — "People have a right to know the truth"
  • Control Room Reaction — producers and viewers watching in horror
  • The Title Reveal — "DISCLOSURE DAY" with the tagline "All Will Be Disclosed"
"People have a right to know the truth. It belongs to 7 billion people."
— Josh O'Connor in the trailer

What the Trailer Tells Us

The teaser reveals Disclosure Day isn't about first contact in the traditional sense — it's about involuntary disclosure. Emily Blunt's character becomes the unwitting conduit for alien communication, broadcast live to millions.

This creates immediate dramatic tension: the secret is out, and the world watches it happen in real-time.

Marketing Campaign

Before the trailer, billboards appeared in New York and Los Angeles bearing only the tagline: "All Will Be Disclosed" — building mystery before the title was even revealed.

What's Next

A full theatrical trailer is expected in early 2026, likely revealing more of the plot, additional cast members, and John Williams' score.
