The "Seven Billion" Theory

The most discussed moment in the Disclosure Day trailer is O'Connor's delivery of a single line: "seven billion."

The problem? Earth's population is currently over 8 billion people. This discrepancy has spawned one of the most viral fan theories about the film.

The Theory: Ethan Hale, through his investigation, learns the true count of humans on Earth‚Äîseven billion. The remaining billion+ are something else. Visitors who have been among us, perhaps for generations.

Whether intentional or not, this single line has generated enormous discussion online. Spielberg is known for meticulous attention to detail, leading fans to believe the discrepancy is deliberate.
