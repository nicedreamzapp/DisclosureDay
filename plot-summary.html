Complete plot summary and story breakdown

Plot Overview

Disclosure Day is Steven Spielberg's 2026 science fiction film about humanity's first contact with extraterrestrial life. The film explores what happens when the existence of alien visitors can no longer be hidden from the public.

The story centers on a Kansas City weather woman (Emily Blunt), a local TV meteorologist who detects an anomalous signal while monitoring weather patterns. This discovery sets off a chain of events that will change humanity forever.

What We Know About The Story

The Discovery

Emily Blunt's character, a Kansas City weather woman, detects an unusual signal while working. This signal doesn't match any known natural or man-made pattern. When she investigates, she makes contact with something beyond human understanding.

The Possession/Communication

After her discovery, Sarah begins changing. She speaks in clicking sounds and unknown languages. Her eyes display strange patterns. Whether she's being possessed or has become a willing translator for the visitors remains unclear.

The Cover-Up

The government has known about extraterrestrial contact for years. Journalist Ethan Hale (Josh O'Connor) discovers the extent of the cover-up and must decide how to expose the truth to "seven billion people."

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Disclosure Day about?
Disclosure Day is about humanity's first contact with extraterrestrial life. A Kansas City weather woman (Emily Blunt) becomes the first human to communicate with the visitors, while a journalist (Josh O'Connor) works to expose decades of cover-ups.
Is Disclosure Day based on a true story?
No, Disclosure Day is a fictional film. However, it draws inspiration from real UAP Congressional hearings and whistleblower testimonies that have occurred in recent years.
Is Disclosure Day a horror movie?
Disclosure Day is a science fiction drama/thriller, not a horror movie. While it has tense and mysterious elements, Spielberg has described it as being about "wonder" rather than fear.
Is Disclosure Day appropriate for kids?
The official rating hasn't been announced. Based on the trailer, expect PG-13 for intense sci-fi sequences and thematic elements. Spielberg's E.T. and Close Encounters were both family-friendly.
How does Disclosure Day end?
The ending has not been revealed. Based on Spielberg's filmography (E.T., Close Encounters), fans expect a hopeful resolution, but the title "Disclosure Day" suggests the world will never be the same.
