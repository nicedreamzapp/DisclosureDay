Spielberg's Alien Filmography

Disclosure Day is Spielberg's 4th film dealing directly with extraterrestrial contact:

  1. Close Encounters of the Third Kind (1977) — Wonder and communication
  2. E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial (1982) — Friendship and loss
  3. War of the Worlds (2005) — Terror and survival
  4. Disclosure Day (2026) — Truth and revelation

Each film represents a different approach to the same question: What happens when we meet them?
