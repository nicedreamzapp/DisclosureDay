News Timeline

Every announcement and update on Disclosure Day

2026
June 12, 2026
Theatrical Release
Disclosure Day opens in theaters and IMAX worldwide.
Spring 2026 (Expected)
Full Trailer Release
A longer trailer expected to reveal more plot details.
Spring 2026 (Expected)
Press Tour
Cast and Spielberg expected to begin international press tour.
January 7, 2026
Colin Firth First Look Released
USA Today reveals first look image of Colin Firth with electrodes attached to his head. The image raises questions about his character's role in the disclosure narrative.
Source: USA Today, World of Reel
2025
December 16, 2025
Title Revealed: "Disclosure Day" + Teaser Trailer
The official title is confirmed as "Disclosure Day" alongside the release of the teaser trailer, which debuted attached to Avatar: Fire and Ash. The weather broadcast scene immediately goes viral.
Source: Deadline, Hollywood Reporter
December 2025
Mystery Billboards Appear
Billboards with the tagline "All Will Be Disclosed" and the date 6.12.26 appear in New York and Los Angeles with no other context, sparking online speculation.
October 2025
John Williams Confirmed as Composer
The legendary composer comes out of retirement for his 30th collaboration with Spielberg. Williams had previously stated he would only return to scoring "when Steven calls."
Source: Variety
May 2025
Principal Photography Wraps
Filming concludes after approximately three months. The film enters post-production.
February 26, 2025
Filming Begins
Principal photography starts in New Jersey under the working title "Non-View". Production will move between New Jersey, Atlanta, New York City, and Huntington.
Source: Production Weekly
2024
2024
Cast Announced
Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson, Wyatt Russell, and Henry Lloyd-Hughes join the ensemble cast.
Source: Deadline, Variety
2024
David Koepp Writing Screenplay
Spielberg taps his Jurassic Park and War of the Worlds collaborator David Koepp to write the screenplay based on Spielberg's original story.
2024
Project Announced
Steven Spielberg announces his return to the UFO genre with a new original film. It will be his first movie since The Fabelmans (2022).
Source: Deadline
