As a Universal Pictures release, Disclosure Day will stream exclusively on Peacock after its theatrical window. Expected late July/August 2026. Peacock Premium required ($7.99/month).

Netflix Not Available

Why Theater First?

Disclosure Day is receiving a traditional theatrical window (the period before streaming availability). Here's why Universal and Spielberg chose this approach:

  • Box office potential - Spielberg films consistently perform well theatrically
  • Cultural event - The first major UFO film from Spielberg in decades
  • Technical showcase - IMAX and Dolby formats enhance the experience
  • Audience demand - Fans want to see this on the big screen
  • Award consideration - Theatrical releases are required for major awards

International Release

Disclosure Day will release internationally on or around June 12, 2026. Some territories may have earlier or later release dates. Check local listings for specific dates in your country.

The film will be released with subtitles and dubbing in multiple languages. IMAX and premium format availability varies by location.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I stream Disclosure Day?
Disclosure Day will be available to stream on Peacock after its theatrical run. As a Universal Pictures release, it's expected to hit Peacock approximately 45-60 days after theatrical release (estimated late July/August 2026).
Is Disclosure Day on Netflix?
No, Disclosure Day will not be on Netflix. The film is distributed by Universal Pictures and will stream exclusively on Peacock after its theatrical window ends.
Will Disclosure Day be on Disney Plus?
No, Disclosure Day will not be on Disney Plus. The film is a Universal Pictures release, so it will stream on Peacock, not Disney Plus.
When does Disclosure Day come to theaters?
Disclosure Day releases in theaters on June 12, 2026. It will be available in IMAX, Dolby Cinema, and standard formats.
Can I rent Disclosure Day digitally?
Yes, Disclosure Day will be available for digital rental on platforms like Amazon Prime Video, Apple TV, Vudu, and Google Play approximately 3-4 weeks after theatrical release.
Is Disclosure Day in IMAX?
Yes, Disclosure Day will be released in IMAX format. Select scenes may feature an expanded aspect ratio for IMAX screens.
