Colman Domingo

General Raymond Pierce

Entertainment Weekly November 2025 Exclusive
Colman Domingo: "I Cried Reading the Disclosure Day Script"

Key Quotes

"I cried reading this script. Multiple times. The humanity in it... it's not what you expect from an alien movie."
On first reading the screenplay
"General Pierce has to make decisions that will affect every person on Earth. The weight of that is immense. And he knows history will judge him."
On his character
"Steven asked me one question in our first meeting: 'What would you do if you knew for certain we weren't alone?' My answer became the foundation for how I played Pierce."
On working with Spielberg
