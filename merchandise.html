Collector Tips

Pre-Order Strategy

For high-demand items like limited edition posters, steelbooks, and collector's editions:

  • Follow official Universal and Amblin social media accounts
  • Sign up for retailer notifications (Target Circle, Best Buy, etc.)
  • Join collector communities on Reddit for early announcements
  • Pre-order early - limited items sell out quickly

Exclusive Hunting

Many retailers offer exclusive variants:

  • Target: Often has exclusive Funko Pops with unique designs
  • Best Buy: Exclusive steelbook artwork
  • Walmart: Exclusive action figure variants
  • Hot Topic: Glow-in-the-dark and flocked Funko exclusives

Beware of Counterfeits

Only buy from authorized retailers. Counterfeit merchandise is common for major releases. Check for official licensing information and be wary of suspiciously low prices.

For Serious Collectors

If you're building a serious Disclosure Day collection, consider:

Documentation

Keep receipts and certificates of authenticity. For valuable items, consider professional grading services.

Storage

Protect investments with proper storage - UV-protective frames for posters, climate-controlled environments for figures.

Insurance

For high-value collections, document items with photos and consider collectibles insurance.
