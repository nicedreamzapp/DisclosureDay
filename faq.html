What is Disclosure Day about?

+

Disclosure Day is a sci-fi thriller about humanity's response to the revelation that extraterrestrial life exists. The film follows multiple characters - including Emily Blunt's lead role - as they grapple with first contact and its political, personal, and existential implications.

The title "Disclosure Day" refers to the moment this information becomes public knowledge. The film explores what happens when the greatest secret in human history can no longer be hidden.

Who directed Disclosure Day?

+

Disclosure Day is directed by Steven Spielberg, the legendary filmmaker behind E.T., Jurassic Park, Schindler's List, Saving Private Ryan, and many other classics.

This marks Spielberg's return to alien-themed filmmaking for the first time since War of the Worlds (2005).

Who wrote Disclosure Day?

+

Disclosure Day was written by David Koepp, a frequent Spielberg collaborator. Koepp previously wrote:

• Jurassic Park
• The Lost World: Jurassic Park
• War of the Worlds
• Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull

Is John Williams doing the music?

+

John Williams' involvement has not been officially confirmed. At 93, Williams announced his retirement from film scoring after Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny (2023).

However, Williams stated he would consider returning for Spielberg projects. Given their 50+ year partnership, many fans hope he'll contribute to Disclosure Day.

Who is the cinematographer?

+

Janusz Kaminski is the cinematographer for Disclosure Day. He has been Spielberg's exclusive DP since Schindler's List (1993), winning two Academy Awards for his work.

Is Disclosure Day a sequel to Close Encounters?

+

Disclosure Day is not officially a sequel to Close Encounters of the Third Kind (1977). It's an original story.

However, many fans speculate thematic connections to Spielberg's earlier alien films (Close Encounters, E.T., War of the Worlds). Some theorize Disclosure Day could exist in the same universe or serve as a spiritual successor.
