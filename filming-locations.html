Where Was Disclosure Day Filmed?

Complete guide to filming locations

Primary Filming Locations

New Jersey Primary Location

The majority of Disclosure Day was filmed in and around New Jersey. The production was kept under tight wraps with security measures to prevent plot leaks. New Jersey's diverse landscapes and proximity to NYC made it ideal for the production.

Production Details:

  • Working Title: "Non-View" - used to maintain secrecy
  • Start Date: February 26, 2025
  • Wrap: May 2025
Atlanta, Georgia Major Location

Atlanta served as another major filming hub for Disclosure Day. Georgia's film-friendly tax incentives and state-of-the-art production facilities have made it a popular destination for major blockbusters.

New York City Urban Scenes

New York City provided the backdrop for key urban sequences in Disclosure Day. The city's iconic skyline and diverse neighborhoods offered authentic settings for the story's exploration of how ordinary people respond to extraordinary revelations.

Huntington, New York Location Filming

Huntington on Long Island served as another filming location for the production. This suburban setting likely provides the grounded, everyday American backdrop that Spielberg often uses to contrast with extraordinary events.

Production Secrecy

Disclosure Day was one of the most secretive Spielberg productions in years. The production employed several measures to prevent leaks:

  • Working title "Non-View" - Used to disguise the project during filming
  • Tight security - Strict set access and NDA enforcement
  • Multiple locations - Spreading production across states made tracking difficult
  • Limited script access - Cast reportedly received pages only as needed
  • Controlled media - Very few set photos leaked during the 3-month shoot
East Coast Focus: Unlike Spielberg's previous alien films shot in deserts and western locations, Disclosure Day's East Coast setting (NJ, NYC, Atlanta) suggests a more grounded, urban-suburban approach—ordinary American neighborhoods confronting extraordinary events.

Set Design

Production designer reports suggest several major sets were constructed:

  • Weather monitoring station - Sarah Chen's workplace where she receives the signal
  • Government command center - Where officials coordinate the response
  • Classified research facility - The "mind control" equipment location
  • Contact site - The location where disclosure occurs

Spielberg's preference for practical sets over pure CGI environments suggests these locations will feel grounded and tactile, enhancing the realism of extraordinary events.

Visual Style

Cinematographer Janusz Kaminski (Spielberg's long-time collaborator) has developed a distinctive visual approach for his alien films:

  • Practical lighting - Real light sources over artificial setups
  • Lens flares - Strategic use to suggest otherworldly presence
  • Low-light capability - Maintaining atmosphere in dark scenes
  • Widescreen composition - Emphasizing vast landscapes and sky

Frequently Asked Questions

Where was Disclosure Day filmed?
Disclosure Day was filmed primarily in New Jersey, Atlanta, New York City, and Huntington (Long Island). Principal photography ran from February to May 2025.
Why was New Jersey chosen for filming?
New Jersey offered diverse suburban and urban landscapes, proximity to New York City, and the production facilities needed for a major blockbuster. The state provides excellent backdrops for "ordinary America."
When did Disclosure Day film?
Principal photography began February 26, 2025 and wrapped in May 2025. The film was shot under the working title "Non-View" to maintain secrecy.
What was the working title of Disclosure Day?
"Non-View" was the working title used during production to keep the project under wraps. The official title "Disclosure Day" was revealed on December 16, 2025.
