Where to Stream Disclosure Day

Theaters First

Exclusive theatrical release June 12, 2026. Streaming comes later.

Theatrical Window

Disclosure Day is a Universal Pictures release, meaning it follows their standard theatrical-to-streaming pipeline. As a prestige Spielberg film, expect an extended theatrical window before home release.

Streaming Platforms

Peacock (Confirmed)

As a Universal film, Disclosure Day will stream on Peacock after its theatrical and VOD window. Universal films typically hit Peacock 45-60 days after theatrical release.

Premium VOD (Expected)

Expect PVOD (rent/buy) availability on Amazon, Apple TV, Vudu, and Google Play approximately 45 days after theatrical release (~late July 2026).

Netflix / Other Platforms

Universal's current deal sends films to Peacock first. Netflix or other platforms would come much later, if at all.

Why See It in Theaters?
