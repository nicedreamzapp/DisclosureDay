The Visionaries

The master storytellers behind Disclosure Day

Steven Spielberg

Director & Story

The filmmaker who defined the UFO genre returns. From Close Encounters of the Third Kind (1977) to E.T. (1982) to War of the Worlds (2005), Spielberg has shaped how humanity imagines first contact.

Now, with Disclosure Day, he asks a different question: What happens when the truth can no longer be hidden?

This marks his 37th feature film as director.

David Koepp

Screenwriter

Spielberg's trusted collaborator on Jurassic Park, The Lost World, War of the Worlds, and Indiana Jones. The architect of blockbuster tension.

Kristie Macosko Krieger

Producer

Five-time Academy Award nominee. 27 years as Spielberg's producing partner. The force behind The Fabelmans, West Side Story, Ready Player One, and now Disclosure Day.

Janusz Kaminski

Cinematographer

Two-time Oscar winner (Schindler's List, Saving Private Ryan). The eye behind every Spielberg film since 1993. Visual poetry meets alien contact.

John Williams

Composer

The 93-year-old legend said he retired. Then Steven called. The five-note motif of Close Encounters may get a spiritual successor.
