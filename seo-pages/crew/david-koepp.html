The Writing Process

Spielberg wrote a 40-50 page story treatment for Disclosure Day — his own original idea about UFO disclosure. He then brought in Koepp, his frequent collaborator, to develop it into a full screenplay.

Koepp confirmed in 2025 that production had wrapped, meaning his screenplay has been fully realized on screen.

Why Koepp for Disclosure Day

Koepp specializes in adapting high-concept premises into emotionally grounded screenplays. His War of the Worlds work with Spielberg proves he can handle alien subject matter while keeping the focus on human characters and family dynamics.

His experience with Jurassic Park showed his ability to balance spectacle with scientific ideas and moral questions — exactly what a disclosure narrative requires.
