The Sound Design Clue

Perhaps the most significant hint is that we hear the aliens before we see them. The clicks and sounds from Blunt's broadcast could be:

  • Communication: An alien language being broadcast through human hosts
  • Signal: Not language at all, but data or coordinates
  • Presence: The sound of their consciousness interfacing with our reality
  • Warning: An announcement of arrival or intent
