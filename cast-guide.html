Frequently Asked Questions

Who is in the Disclosure Day cast?
The main cast includes Emily Blunt, Josh O'Connor, Colin Firth, Colman Domingo, Eve Hewson, and Wyatt Russell. Steven Spielberg directs from a screenplay by David Koepp.
Who does Emily Blunt play in Disclosure Day?
Emily Blunt plays Dr. Sarah Chen, a meteorologist who becomes the first human to make contact with extraterrestrial visitors. Her character appears to become a vessel for alien communication.
What is Josh O'Connor's role in Disclosure Day?
Josh O'Connor plays Agent Marcus Webb, a government whistleblower who works to expose the truth about alien contact. He delivers the film's famous "seven billion" line.
Is John Williams composing the music for Disclosure Day?
Yes, legendary composer John Williams is scoring Disclosure Day, continuing his decades-long collaboration with Steven Spielberg on films including E.T., Close Encounters, and Jurassic Park.
Who wrote the Disclosure Day screenplay?
David Koepp wrote the screenplay. Koepp previously collaborated with Spielberg on Jurassic Park, The Lost World, War of the Worlds, and Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull.
