Tips for Getting Tickets

Pro Tips:
  • Sign up for alerts - Fandango and theater apps will notify you when tickets go live
  • Join loyalty programs - AMC Stubs, Regal Crown Club often get early access
  • IMAX sells fast - Premium format tickets for opening weekend sell out first
  • Book early shows - Matinees on opening day often have availability
  • Try multiple sites - If one site crashes, try another retailer
  • Have payment ready - Save your card info for faster checkout

Theater Loyalty Programs

  • AMC Stubs A-List - $19.95/month for 3 movies/week including IMAX
  • Regal Unlimited - $18-23/month for unlimited movies
  • Cinemark Movie Club - $9.99/month for 1 movie + discounts
  • Alamo Season Pass - Unlimited movies at Alamo Drafthouse

Frequently Asked Questions

When do Disclosure Day tickets go on sale?
Disclosure Day tickets are expected to go on sale in late April or early May 2026, approximately 4-6 weeks before the June 12, 2026 release date.
Where can I buy Disclosure Day tickets?
Tickets will be available at Fandango, AMC Theatres, Regal, Cinemark, Atom Tickets, and directly from local theater websites.
Will there be Thursday night previews?
Most likely yes. Major releases typically have Thursday evening preview screenings starting around 5-7 PM before the official Friday release.
How much will Disclosure Day tickets cost?
Standard tickets typically range from $12-16. IMAX and Dolby Cinema tickets cost $18-25. 4DX and special formats can be $25-35.
Should I buy IMAX tickets?
For a Spielberg film with John Williams' score and epic visuals, IMAX or Dolby Cinema is highly recommended for the first viewing to get the full experience.
